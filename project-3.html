Project 01:
Hannanymous Now Live

Web Development for Freelance Photographer

Project Overview

Hannaymous based in Orlanda, Florida. Shooting for the likes of The Weeknd and Kanye West requried a bespoke website which was unique to any photography site ever before.

Project Goals

Unique and custom layout with features Immediate Theatre required a redesign of their existing site that is easy to navigate, showcase their purpose and appeal to many different user types.

Outcome

The outcome of the site was great, with a lot of traction in the first day hitting over 1000 visits within less than 24 hours. As the site continues to gain support, development will be improved to be even better along the way.
